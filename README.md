## What Aescripts Faux Lock Does

Aescripts Faux Lock is an After Effects productivity plugin made for motion designers who want tighter control while working inside crowded compositions. Its main job is temporary layer protection, so selected layers can stay untouched while you keep animating, rearranging, and polishing the rest of the scene.

That approach is particularly useful in larger motion graphics timelines, where even a small misclick can slow things down. Faux Lock keeps the workflow simple and lightweight, so it blends into day-to-day composition tasks without piling on extra steps.

## How to Use It

A typical workflow looks like this:

1. Launch a composition in Adobe After Effects.
2. Select the layers you want to keep from being edited.
3. Apply Faux Lock to temporarily protect those layers.
4. Keep animating, adjusting, or organizing the rest of the comp.
5. Turn protection off when you are ready to work on those layers again.

For the best experience, use it on heavy timelines, shared comps, or projects where selecting the wrong layer happens often.
